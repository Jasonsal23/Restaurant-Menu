Origin Story

Las Vegas is home and that is where I am glad to call H-Thai-ML home. It began when my parents moved from Thailand and began the family restaurant growing my love for the kitchen. I later took over the main location in Las Vegas and with an growing demand of the food, I began to chain in different cities throughout the state of Nevada. There is no better place to come get some of the best Thai food in the state of Nevada.
